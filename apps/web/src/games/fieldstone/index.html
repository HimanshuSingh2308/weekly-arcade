📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Build and defend your kingdom! Place terrain tiles to harvest resources, then use those resources to defend against enemy waves. Survive 10 waves to win!

🧩 Gameplay Loop

1. Place Tiles: Tetris-style pieces fall from the top. Move and rotate them to fill the board.

2. Complete Rows: Fill an entire row to harvest it! Unlike Tetris, rows STAY on the board as permanent kingdom land (shown with golden glow). You gain resources based on the terrain types.

3. Survive Attacks: After placing 10 tiles, enemies attack! Your defence (from walls + units) must beat enemy power or you lose tiles.

4. Shop & Upgrade: Between waves, buy cards to boost resource production or add defence units.

Key Insight: Group same terrain types together before completing a row for bonus resources!

🌍 Terrain Types

🌲 Forest = 🪵 Lumber
🌾 Plains = 🌾 Food
⛰️ Mountain = 🪨 Stone
💧 River = 💰 Gold
🏠 Settlement = All

💡 Pro Tips

  • Place same terrain types adjacent to each other before completing a row = more resources!
  • Clear multiple rows at once for big score bonuses (2 rows = 30pts, 3 = 60pts, 4 = 100pts!)
  • Buy unit cards (🗡️🏹🐴) before tough waves - they add to your defence
  • Building cards give permanent bonuses (Lumber Mill = +50% lumber forever)
  • Watch the "NEXT" preview to plan where to place your current piece
